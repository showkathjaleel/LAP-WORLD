Maximum heat dissipation for the pinnacle of performance

Cooling is one of the greatest challenges for high-performance laptops, and ProArt Studiobook is the first ASUS creator laptop to incorporate fully-automated, machine-applied liquid metal technology. The liquid metal is applied between the CPU heat sink, and it’s extremely effective at transferring thermal energy. Manually-applied liquid metal can short-circuit the motherboard if it leaks onto any of the circuitry. Dust, debris and other human-induced contamination during the application process can also impair connections near the CPU cooler. With our fully-automated process the liquid metal is applied evenly and precisely only where it’s needed. This ensures maximum heat dissipation and also the reliable operation of your laptop.
During rigorous testing, ASUS engineers have observed a performance boost of up to 9.4% in the Cinebench score, and a 29% improvement in the package power wattage5 when liquid metal is used with the latest 12th Gen Intel® Core™ i9 CPUs. Its ability to quickly lower CPU temperature helps processors sustain higher clock speeds for longer, and helps to prevent fans from ramping up and creating unwanted noise.
